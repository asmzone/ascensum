WHAT IS CONCILIUM?

In the world of cryptocurrencies, the price or value of an asset is supported by 3 key aspects (SUPPLY, DEMAND and HODL) that move within a structure with their own orbit, but these 3 aspects are also influenced by other factors (macroeconomic, market sentiment, TVL, etc.), trying to maintain or stabilize the price is like the 3-body problem (unsolved scientific theory), it is as if we wanted to stabilize a scale with 3 plates, however, what happens if an external intelligence influences the entire structure?

If we study the cryptoasset market, such as Bitcoin, it has characteristics that allow it to be the reference (external intelligence) for other cryptocurrencies, allowing it to have a balance point, however, its price is volatile compared to any other market other than the cryptocurrency market, but compared to other cryptoassets it is less volatile, why? Because it has a greater intention to HODL, greater DEMAND and lower SUPPLY.

CONCILIUM is a disruptive Blockchain ecosystem that seeks, more than having an asset with monetary value, to generate intangible value in its existence and evokes the reconciliation between the 3 key aspects, offering the market a system that harmonizes and puts them into operation that generates benefits for those who make up the ecosystem, investors, holders and traders.

CONCILIUM includes a protocol to inject external capital into the liquidity of its token ($CONCILIUM) beyond its behavior in the market, in this way it guarantees benefits to investors.

By complying with all the parameters of CONCILIUM, we could achieve the ambitious goal of quintuple or multiply the investment by 10, that is the reason for this initiative, to have a conciliatory agent over all the factors that intervene in the depreciation of a cryptoasset, preventing its value from falling, turning the $CONCILIUM token into a bet with a high degree of certainty.
